What is Computer Vision?

Computer vision is a field of AI where machines are trained to understand images and videos, just like humans do. It's behind facial recognition, medical imaging, self-driving cars, and much more.

Why It Matters

Imagine an app that can spot a brain tumor in seconds or a camera that helps the blind “see” using audio. That’s computer vision. It’s not just technology, it’s real-world impact.
